== Description ==
Provides a two player backgammon board play on your website. Uses an HTML-Anchor element.

== Features ==
* Backgammon online with friends.
* Play head-to-head with other real players.
* Play variations include Nackgammon, Longgammon, and Hypergammon.
* Download finished matches for self analysis using Jellyfish formats.
* Supports the standard player ELO rating system.
* Play single and multi-game Matches.
* Match-play lengths up to 101.
* Play with or without the Crawford rule.
* Play with or without the doubling cube.
* Play with or without the a play timer. 3 different play speeds supported.
* Compatible with desktop and mobile devices. Play anywhere, anytime.
* Integration with WordPress via shortcode or widget.

== Installation ==
Dowload the plugin to your WP site.
Enabled the plugin.
Add the [backgammon_playboard] shortcode to a post or page content.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this include Backgammon Tournament Management? =

No. Backgammon tournaments are only available via organized efforts provided by a tournament director person. Please contact simplybg@feweb.net for more details.

= Can I play for free? =

Yes. Your first few matches are free. The play board is offered via SimplyBG where account registration is free and comes with a free subscription that will allow you to play and try out the board manager.  After a few matches, affordable annual subscription packages are available. See <a href="https://www.simplybg.com/subscribe">https://www.simplybg.com/subscribe</a> for more details.
